About Us

Who We Are

BlueWave International is a leading import-export company based in Surat, Gujarat, India. We deal in a wide range of products including Water Bottles, Agricultural Products, Industrial Chemicals, and Consumer Electronics.

Our mission is to build long-term business relationships through integrity, quality, and timely delivery.

We serve clients in Asia, Europe, Africa, and the Americas with a dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction.
